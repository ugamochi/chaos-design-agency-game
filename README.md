## Features

- **Real-time game timer** - 1 game hour = 1 real second, automatically pauses during conversations
- **Project management** - Assign team members, track progress, handle scope creep
- **Team morale & burnout** - Manage team happiness and player burnout levels
- **Client conversations** - Make decisions that affect money, morale, and satisfaction
- **Hours system** - Weekly hour capacity (40 hours/week), overtime mechanics
- **Illness system** - Random team member sickness affects productivity
- **Office visualization** - Mini Metro-style office layout visualization
- **Multiple victory paths** - Rockstar, Professional, or Survivor endings
- **Message repeat prevention** - Conversations won't spam (except break reminders)
- **Tutorial system** - Learn as you play with guided walkthrough
- **High scores** - Track your best runs and performance

## Tech

Pure JavaScript, HTML, CSS. No dependencies.
