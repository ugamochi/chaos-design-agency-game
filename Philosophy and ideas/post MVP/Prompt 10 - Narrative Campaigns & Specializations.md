PROMPT 10: Narrative Campaigns & Specializations

Add structured story campaigns and agency specialization paths.

REQUIREMENTS:

1. Campaign mode: 3 narrative arcs

Arc 1: "The Startup Grind"
- Start as solo freelancer
- Build to 3-person team
- Choose: Stay small or grow?

Arc 2: "Agency Growing Pains"
- 5-10 person team
- Office politics emerge
- Choose specialization

Arc 3: "Industry Recognition"
- Compete for awards
- Big clients, big stakes
- Choose: Sell out or stay boutique?

2. Specialization system:
Options:
- Boutique Design Studio: High-end, low volume
- Dev Shop: Technical focus, faster projects
- Full-Service Agency: Generalist, more chaos
- Brand Specialists: Premium pricing, demanding clients
- Startup Partner: Equity deals, long-term risk

Each specialization:
- Unique client types
- Different project structures
- Specialized team members
- Distinct challenges

3. Character story arcs:
- Team members have personal goals
- Help them achieve goals = loyalty bonus
- Ignore them = risk losing them
- Multi-chapter narratives

4. Industry events:
- Award ceremonies (stress + prestige)
- Conferences (networking opportunities)
- Economic shifts (boom/recession cycles)
- Trend changes (flat design era, AI hype, etc.)

Make players feel like they're building something, not just surviving.
